Slate Gun Death Data of US

Manual:
1. Hover Over a States to see the information of the deaths of that state.
2. Click on the Buttons to see deaths in terms of male/female/all together
3. Clicking on any states a bar chart and a pie chart will be generated below the map.
4. Bar Chart shows infomation of deaths of the cities in that state
5. Pie Chart shows information of deaths by state based on age group
